Food Orders and Payment Policy

At Anita's Restaurant, your privacy and satisfaction are our top priorities. This policy outlines the terms regarding how food orders are processed and the available payment options.

Pick-Up Only Policy

All food ordered from Anita's Restaurant is available for pick-up only. We do not offer delivery services at this time. When placing an order online or over the phone, please be aware that you will need to visit our location to collect your food at the designated pick-up time.

Order Pick-Up Instructions

Once your order has been placed, you will receive notifcation by email when order is ready for pickup. Upon arrival at Anita's Restaurant, please visit our front counter to pick up your order. Kindly provide your name and order details to our staff to ensure a smooth handover.

Payment Options

  • Pay On-Site: You may pay for your order in person when you arrive to pick it up. We accept cash, credit, and debit cards at the restaurant.

    • Refunds and Cancellations

    If you need to cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. Orders canceled before preparation has started are eligible for a full refund. However, once food preparation has begun, we may not be able to process a refund.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about our food ordering process or payment methods, please feel free to contact us at (316) 321-1301 or visit us at 2529 W Central Ave, El Dorado, KS 67042.
